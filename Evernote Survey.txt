Evernote

Pros
1. Allows creation and update of notes over various platforms (even forwarding emails to evernote email account).
2. Allows a variety of notes to created and classified e.g. business cards, recipes, instruction mannuals, website clippings.
3. Incorporates itself into different platforms for the ease of search and notifying user e.g. Google search.

Cons
1. Depends heavily on the internet i.e. user is cut off from the database if the internet is down.
2. Only 1-level notebooks and stacks are allowed unlike a file system i.e. the ability to organize notes is limited
